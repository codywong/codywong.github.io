Sending Payment Reminders

Team Venmo
  • 6 hours ago
  • Updated
Follow

To remind someone of a payment to your Venmo account from a computer, click here and click "Add Debit or Credit Card" then add your card information.

If you’re in the app please follow the instructions below:

  1. Tap the "☰" icon in the upper left hand corner of the app
  2. Tap the “Incomplete” entry
  3. In the requests tab, click remind for the transaction you would like to send a reminder for.

Once you click remind, a notification will be sent to the payee. Once they pay you, feel free to continue adding comments and emojis!

Note: You can only send a reminder once to prevent spamming. After that, you might need to reach out them through other methods such as texting and calling.
